<p><strong>The PEX Pipes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PE-Xa Pipe</li><li>PE-Xb Pipe</li><li>PE-Xc Pipe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PEX pipes market is primarily segmented into three types: PE-Xa, PE-Xb, and PE-Xc. PE-Xa is produced through an advanced high-temperature process, offering excellent flexibility and resistance to cracking, making it ideal for plumbing applications. PE-Xb is manufactured using a chemical cross-linking method, known for its good thermal stability and lower production costs, suitable for a variety of applications. PE-Xc, produced via electron beam irradiation, provides superior mechanical strength and resistance to chemicals, widely used in heating systems.</p></p>
