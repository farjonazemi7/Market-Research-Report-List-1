<p><p>The Vane Thermo Anemometer market comprises three main types: Constant Current Anemometers, which maintain a steady current to measure airflow by monitoring temperature changes; Constant Pressure Anemometers, which measure airflow by keeping a consistent pressure differential across the vane; and Thermostatic Anemometers, which utilize a thermal sensor to detect temperature variations caused by air movement. Each type serves specific applications in assessing airflow and environmental conditions, offering precise measurements for HVAC, laboratory, and industrial settings.</p></p>
