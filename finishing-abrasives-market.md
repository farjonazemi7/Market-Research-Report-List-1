<p><p>Finishing abrasives are crucial in the manufacturing and metalworking industries, categorized primarily into bonded and coated types. Bonded abrasives consist of abrasive grains held together by a binding agent, providing durability and precision in grinding applications. Coated abrasives, on the other hand, feature abrasive particles adhered to a flexible backing material, making them ideal for sanding and finishing surfaces. Both types play essential roles in achieving desired surface finishes, enhancing product quality, and improving operational efficiency across various applications.</p></p>
