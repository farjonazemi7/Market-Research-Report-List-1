<p><p>The Rotary Changeover Switches market is categorized by the number of poles they possess, which determines their functionality in electrical systems. A 1-pole switch controls a single circuit, making it suitable for basic applications. A 2-pole switch manages two circuits, often used for single-phase motors. A 3-pole switch is designed for three-phase systems, facilitating balanced load management. Lastly, a 4-pole switch can handle three-phase plus neutral, providing enhanced versatility for complex electrical setups, catering to varied industrial and residential needs.</p></p>
